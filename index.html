Questions people ask us

Do you give free estimates?

In most cases, yes — we’ll talk through your project, look at photos, and schedule a site visit if needed before giving you a written estimate.

What types of jobs do you take?

We handle everything from small repairs and punch-list items up to full interior remodels, exterior repairs, and rental make-ready work.

How do payments work?

Small jobs are usually paid in full at completion. Bigger projects are split into phases with deposits so we can schedule labor and materials without surprises.

Are you insured?

Yes — we treat your home like it’s our own and carry the coverage we need to protect both you and us while we work.
